Champions League Group Winner Probability

Each team is placed here by the median probability, via oddschecker, that it will win its Champions League Group.

It's not valid to compare teams across lines; this chart doesn't, for example, suggest that Chelsea is better than Bayern. Instead, it says that Chelsea is considered more likely to win their group than Bayern is, due to being part of a much weaker group of teams.

The groups led by Dortmund and Atletico Madrid immediately jump out here as the ones about which the betting community is unsure. No team in these groups except for Marseille and Vienna pays more than 4 to 1, while every other group has 2 or 3 that pay more.
